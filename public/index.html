About Us

We are here to get rid of your distractions, and surrond you with motivation to reach your end goal, Sucess!

Well, what makes us differnt from any other motivation app? let me tell you. We have key features which can be customized to fit your needs and features that can be utilized by all.

Collaborate

    with the youth community

What a better way to study then with study groups! Make, join and particpate in live or custom calls with your family and freinds. This can help you be motivated in your goals with the help of a live cheersquad! It is benficial to reduce stress and anixety while studying or doing a task. Doing it with a help of freind can make it easier. Work together towards your goal!

Organize

    your tasks and goals

Manage each others tasks and be in charge of your own and each other's to-do lists and calendars. Goal setting system feature in which group members can view each other's goals. Motivate each other to complete tasks with features such as app locking, timers, and reminders

Motivate

    To get it done together

Accountability within group on goals
-Incentives won through a point system, based on in-app progress, with real prizes
-Partner up with companies to use their products as incentives

How are we different from the rest?

  • Focus on youth socialization, motivation and mental health
  • A reward system
  • Individual and group goal setting, progress tracking, video calling, group studying and accountability
  • School and educational partnerships
  • Completely free-to-use app

We stand out from every competitor! We also took into consideration our market validation. Where 80% Students have trouble with motivation, time management, and/or lack of socialization and 90% Students have trouble dealing with their mental health.

Our Revenue Stream

  1. Advertisments

    • Advertisements on app and website

    • As our users grow, so will our revenue off advertisements

  2. Partnerships

    • Partner up with schools and educational institutions

    • White labeled version for app tailored to partner needs

  3. Revenue sharing

    • Revenue sharing with companies involved in incentive program

    • Companies will receive publicity and traction and share revenue in return
